# Death Saving Throws

## Quick Rule

A dying character makes death saving throws to determine whether they stabilize or die unless healing or another rule changes the state first.

## When Used

Use for player characters and important creatures using PC-style 0 HP rules.

## GM Notes

Track successes, failures, damage while dying, and stabilization clearly at the table.

SW5e Player's Handbook, pages 228-229.
