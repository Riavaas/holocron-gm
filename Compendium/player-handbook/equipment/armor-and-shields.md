# Armor and Shields

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 168-170
* Original section: Armor and Shields

## Summary

Armor and shields change Armor Class, stealth, mobility, and proficiency expectations.

## Key Rules

* Armor proficiency matters.
* Armor category affects AC formula and restrictions.
* Shields add defensive value when equipped.
* Donning and doffing armor takes time.

## GM Use

Use this for AC disputes, stealth questions, and armor swaps during rests or preparation.

## Common Rulings

* If armor is not fitted or sized, use the variant sizing rule only if your table tracks it.
* Ask whether the shield is held and ready before granting benefit.
