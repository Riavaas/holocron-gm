# Food, Water, and Resting

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 218-219
* Original section: Food and Water, Resting

## Summary

Food, water, and rest govern attrition. Short rests provide limited recovery, while long rests represent a larger reset when the party has enough safety and time.

## Key Rules

* Food and water matter most during travel, survival pressure, captivity, and harsh environments.
* A short rest is a limited downtime break for recovery and features that recharge on short rest.
* A long rest is extended downtime and is the main recovery point for many resources.
* Rest can be interrupted or restricted by danger, armor variants, or hostile conditions.

## GM Use

Use rest rules to manage pacing. Safe rest changes the adventure rhythm; unsafe rest creates choices and consequences.

## Common Rulings

* Ask where the party rests, who watches, and what makes the location safe.
* Do not punish every rest attempt, but make hostile areas feel hostile.
* Track food and water when scarcity is part of the scene.
