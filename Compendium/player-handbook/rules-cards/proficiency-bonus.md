# Proficiency Bonus

## Quick Rule

Add proficiency bonus when a rule, skill, tool, save, weapon, or feature says the character is proficient.

## When Used

Use on trained checks, proficient saves, attacks with proficient weapons, and class or feature calculations.

## GM Notes

Do not add proficiency just because a character is good at something; add it when training or a rule applies.

SW5e Player's Handbook, pages 208-209.
