# Difficult Terrain

## Quick Rule

Difficult terrain makes movement cost more and can slow travel or tactical repositioning.

## When Used

Use for rubble, crowds, deep snow, mud, swamp, wreckage, narrow machinery spaces, or unstable ground.

## GM Notes

Announce difficult terrain before movement choices when characters can see it.
