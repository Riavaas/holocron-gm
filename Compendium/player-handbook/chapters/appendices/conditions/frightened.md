# Frightened

## Quick Effect

Fear limits approach and imposes pressure while the source is relevant.

## Mechanical Effects

* The frightened creature struggles while the fear source is in play.
* Movement and attacks may be restricted by the source rules.
* Use line of sight and distance carefully.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
