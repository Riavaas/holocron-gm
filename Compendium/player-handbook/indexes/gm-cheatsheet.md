# GM Cheatsheet

## Core d20 Resolution

1. Confirm intent and method.
2. Decide whether the outcome is uncertain and meaningful.
3. Choose check, attack roll, or saving throw.
4. Apply ability modifier, proficiency, advantage/disadvantage, and situational modifiers.
5. Compare to DC, AC, or opposed result.
6. Narrate the consequence and next decision.

## Ability Check Quick Flow

* Active attempt: [Ability Checks](../rules-cards/ability-checks.md)
* Hidden or repeated effort: [Passive Checks](../rules-cards/passive-checks.md)
* Direct opposition: [Contests](../rules-cards/contests.md)
* Static challenge: [Difficulty Classes](../rules-cards/difficulty-classes.md)
* Reactive danger: [Saving Throws](../rules-cards/saving-throws.md)

## Combat Round Quick Flow

1. Establish positions and surprise.
2. Roll [Initiative](../rules-cards/initiative.md).
3. Run turns in order.
4. On each turn, resolve movement, action, bonus action if any, object interaction, and reactions when triggered.
5. Apply damage, healing, conditions, and 0 HP rules immediately.

## Common Combat Actions

* [Actions in Combat](../rules-cards/actions-in-combat.md): Attack, Cast a Power, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Guard, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Use an Object, improvise.
* [Attack Rolls](../rules-cards/attack-rolls.md): confirm target, range, cover, visibility, modifiers, and AC.
* [Cover](../rules-cards/cover.md): judge from the attacker's position.

## Damage and Healing Quick Rules

* Use [Damage and Healing](../rules-cards/damage-and-healing.md) for hit point changes.
* Use [Critical Hits](../rules-cards/critical-hits.md) when an attack crits.
* Use [Dropping to Zero Hit Points](../rules-cards/dropping-to-zero-hit-points.md) immediately at 0 HP.
* Use [Death Saving Throws](../rules-cards/death-saving-throws.md) for dying PCs and important creatures.

## Resting Quick Rules

* [Short Rest](../rules-cards/short-rest.md): limited recovery pause.
* [Long Rest](../rules-cards/long-rest.md): extended recovery in safety.
* Ask where the party rests, who watches, and what enemies do with the time.

## Social and Exploration Quick Rulings

* [Travel Pace](../rules-cards/travel-pace.md): speed versus caution.
* [Difficult Terrain](../rules-cards/difficult-terrain.md): make terrain matter.
* [Vision and Light](../rules-cards/vision-and-light.md): decide what each side can perceive.
* [Social Interaction](../rules-cards/social-interaction.md): NPC motive plus player approach before a roll.

## When Unsure, Use This GM Ruling Pattern

1. State the fictional risk.
2. Pick the closest rule card.
3. Choose a check, save, contest, or attack roll.
4. Set a DC or target.
5. Tell the player the visible stakes.
6. Roll once and move the scene forward.

## Conditions Quick Table

Use [Conditions](../rules-cards/conditions.md) as combat tracker status flags. Track source, duration, save timing, and removal trigger.

## Casting Quick Flow

1. Confirm Force or tech power.
2. Confirm known power, points, level, prerequisites, and casting focus if relevant.
3. Check [Casting Time](../rules-cards/casting-time.md), [Range/Targets](../rules-cards/power-targets.md), and [Duration](../rules-cards/power-duration.md).
4. Resolve [Power Save DC](../rules-cards/power-save-dc.md) or [Power Attack Rolls](../rules-cards/power-attack-rolls.md).
5. Track [Concentration](../rules-cards/concentration.md) and ongoing effects.

## Equipment Quick Rulings

* Armor: confirm proficiency, armor category, shield state, and don/doff timing.
* Weapons: check proficiency, range, ammunition, and [Weapon Properties](../rules-cards/weapon-properties.md).
* Explosives: confirm trigger, placement, area, save or attack, and ally exposure.
* Medical/repair gear: ask what supply or tool is used, then decide if it enables the attempt, lowers DC, or grants proficiency.
