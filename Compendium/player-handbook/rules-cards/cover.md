# Cover

## Quick Rule

Cover protects a target when an obstacle blocks part or all of the attack or effect path.

## When Used

Use around walls, crates, vehicles, doorways, creatures, barricades, wreckage, and elevation.

## GM Notes

Judge cover from the attacker's position. Cover can change when either side moves.
