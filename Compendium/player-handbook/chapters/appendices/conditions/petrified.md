# Petrified

## Quick Effect

Transformed into inert solid matter and effectively removed from normal action.

## Mechanical Effects

* The creature cannot act normally.
* Movement and awareness are effectively suspended.
* Damage and resistance details come from the exact condition text.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
