# Weapons

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 171-174
* Original section: Weapons

## Summary

Weapons are defined by proficiency, category, damage, range, and properties.

## Key Rules

* Proficiency affects attack rolls.
* Weapon properties change how weapons are used.
* Improvised weapons need a GM ruling.
* Range, reload, ammunition, and special traits matter in combat.

## GM Use

Use this for attack setup: weapon, proficiency, ability modifier, range, ammo, and property effects.

## Common Rulings

* If a weapon property is unclear, apply the narrowest reading that preserves the weapon identity.
