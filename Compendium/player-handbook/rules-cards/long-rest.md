# Long Rest

## Quick Rule

A long rest is extended downtime that restores major resources when the party has enough time and safety.

## When Used

Use at major breaks, safe shelters, ships, bases, settlements, or secured camps.

## GM Notes

If the location is unsafe, decide whether the rest is interrupted, delayed, or possible only after precautions.
