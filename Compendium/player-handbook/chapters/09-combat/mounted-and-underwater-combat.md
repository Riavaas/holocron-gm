# Mounted and Underwater Combat

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 229-229
* Original section: Underwater Combat, Mounted Combat

## Summary

Special combat environments change movement, attacks, and control. Underwater combat limits normal tactics, while mounted combat depends on mounting, dismounting, and controlling the mount.

## Key Rules

* Underwater scenes can restrict attacks and movement.
* A creature can mount or dismount when size and positioning allow.
* Controlled mounts and independent mounts behave differently.
* The mount's movement and the rider's actions must be adjudicated together.

## GM Use

Before the fight starts, explain what the environment changes. Players should know if weapons, movement, breath, or mount control are limited.

## Common Rulings

* Treat unusual vehicles or beasts like mounts if the same questions apply.
* Use contests or saves when a rider might be dismounted.
* Underwater visibility and breathing should matter alongside attack limits.
