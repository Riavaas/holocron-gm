# Blinded

## Quick Effect

Cannot see; sight-based tasks fail or suffer, attacks involving the creature are affected.

## Mechanical Effects

* A blinded creature cannot see.
* Sight-dependent checks fail or are heavily impaired.
* Attackers usually gain advantage against it.
* Its own attacks usually suffer disadvantage.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
