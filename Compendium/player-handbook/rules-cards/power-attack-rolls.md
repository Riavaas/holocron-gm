# Power Attack Rolls

## Quick Rule

Power attack rolls use the caster's power attack modifier against a target defense.

## When Used

Use when a power says to make a ranged or melee power attack.

## GM Notes

Check range, cover, visibility, and advantage/disadvantage first.
