# Exhaustion

## Quick Effect

Layered attrition condition from strain, starvation, environment, or forced marches.

## Mechanical Effects

* Track the exhaustion level separately.
* Higher levels become increasingly dangerous.
* Rest or specific treatment can reduce it if conditions allow.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
