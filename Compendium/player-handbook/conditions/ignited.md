# Ignited

## Quick Effect

SW5e condition for being on fire or actively burning.

## Mechanical Effects

* Track ongoing fire effect from the source.
* Use action, save, or environmental solution if allowed.
* Apply damage and duration from the causing rule.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
