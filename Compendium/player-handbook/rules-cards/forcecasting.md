# Forcecasting

## Quick Rule

Forcecasting uses Force powers through a character's forcecasting rules and ability.

## When Used

Use when a character casts a Force power or a feature references Force powers.

## GM Notes

Check known powers, force points, casting ability, save DC, attack modifier, and concentration.
