# Conditions

## Quick Rule

Conditions alter what a creature can perceive, do, resist, or suffer.

## When Used

Use whenever a power, attack, hazard, item, or ruling applies a named status effect.

## GM Notes

Track source, duration, save timing, and removal condition. For live play, put the condition on the combat tracker immediately.
