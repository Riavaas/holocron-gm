# Invisible

## Quick Effect

Cannot be seen without special sense or aid.

## Mechanical Effects

* The creature is unseen for targeting and perception.
* Attack interactions usually use unseen attacker rules.
* Noise, tracks, sensors, and effects can still reveal position.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
