# Medical Supplies

## Quick Rule

Medical supplies support treatment, stabilization, poison response, healing support, and field care.

## When Used

Use when a character uses antitoxkits, medpacs, traumakits, or similar supplies.

## GM Notes

Ask what supply is used and whether the situation needs a check.
