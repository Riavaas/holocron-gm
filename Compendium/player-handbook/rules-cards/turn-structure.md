# Turn Structure

## Quick Rule

On a turn, a creature can move and take one action; bonus actions, reactions, and object interactions apply only when their rules allow.

## When Used

Use every combat round and in other strict turn-order scenes.

## GM Notes

When players hesitate, ask for movement, action, and any bonus action in that order.
