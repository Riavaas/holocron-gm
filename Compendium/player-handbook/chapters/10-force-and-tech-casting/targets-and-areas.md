# Targets and Areas of Effect

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 233-233
* Original section: Targets and Areas of Effect

## Summary

Targets and areas define who or what a power can affect.

## Key Rules

* A power must have a valid target.
* A clear path can matter.
* Areas such as cone, cube, cylinder, line, and sphere shape affected spaces.

## GM Use

Before rolling saves, mark the area and affected creatures.

## Common Rulings

* If grid placement is disputed, choose the origin point and area shape first.
