# Actions in Combat

## Quick Rule

Choose one main action, such as Attack, Cast a Power, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Guard, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Use an Object, or an improvised action.

## When Used

Use on each creature's turn in combat.

## GM Notes

For improvised actions, choose the closest existing action or resolve with a check, save, or contest.
