# Movement

## Quick Rule

Use speed for tactical movement and travel pace for overland or extended movement.

## When Used

Use during exploration, chases, hazards, combat positioning, and travel.

## GM Notes

Ask where the character is going and what path they take; terrain and danger determine whether the move is simple.
