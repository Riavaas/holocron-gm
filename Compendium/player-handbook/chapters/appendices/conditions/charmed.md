# Charmed

## Quick Effect

Cannot harm or target the charmer in hostile ways and is socially influenced.

## Mechanical Effects

* The charmed creature cannot attack the charmer.
* The charmer has social leverage on interaction checks.
* Good for social control, not full mind control.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
