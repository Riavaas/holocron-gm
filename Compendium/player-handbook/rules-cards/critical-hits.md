# Critical Hits

## Quick Rule

A critical hit changes the damage roll according to the critical hit rules for attacks.

## When Used

Use when an attack roll scores a critical hit or a rule expands critical hit behavior.

## GM Notes

Confirm which dice are doubled or added before rolling damage, especially with riders and features.

SW5e Player's Handbook, page 227.
