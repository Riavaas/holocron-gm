# Concentration

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 232-232
* Original section: Concentration

## Summary

Concentration maintains certain powers over time and can be broken by rules or events.

## Key Rules

* Only powers that require concentration use it.
* Losing concentration ends the maintained effect.
* Track who is concentrating and on what.

## GM Use

Put concentration directly on the combat tracker.

## Common Rulings

* If the caster is disrupted, check whether concentration must be tested or ends by rule.
* A caster should not maintain incompatible concentration effects.
