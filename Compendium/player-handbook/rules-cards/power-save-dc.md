# Power Save DC

## Quick Rule

Power save DC is the target number creatures roll against when a power calls for a saving throw.

## When Used

Use whenever a power says the target makes a saving throw.

## GM Notes

Confirm caster ability and proficiency before announcing the DC.

SW5e Player's Handbook, pages 233-233.
