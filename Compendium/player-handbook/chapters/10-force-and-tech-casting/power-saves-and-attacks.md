# Power Saves and Attacks

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 233-233
* Original section: Saving Throws and Attack Rolls

## Summary

Some powers call for saving throws; others require power attack rolls.

## Key Rules

* Save DC comes from the caster formula.
* Power attack modifier comes from the caster formula.
* The power says whether targets save or the caster attacks.

## GM Use

Ask for the power text trigger: save, attack, or automatic effect.

## Common Rulings

* Do not call for both a save and attack unless the power says so.
