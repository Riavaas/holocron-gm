# Social Interaction

## Quick Rule

Roleplay the approach, then use ability checks when the NPC's reaction is uncertain and consequential.

## When Used

Use for negotiation, deception, intimidation, diplomacy, interrogation, bargaining, and investigation through conversation.

## GM Notes

NPC motives matter more than a single roll. Let leverage, relationships, and risk shape the DC and outcome.
