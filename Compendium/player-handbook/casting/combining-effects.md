# Combining Effects

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 233-233
* Original section: Combining Effects

## Summary

Multiple power effects can overlap, but identical or similar effects may not stack freely.

## Key Rules

* Track source and duration.
* Apply the most relevant or strongest effect when rules restrict stacking.
* Avoid double-counting the same named effect.

## GM Use

Use this to keep buffs, debuffs, and control powers clean.

## Common Rulings

* When unsure, let different effects coexist but prevent duplicate named effects from multiplying.
