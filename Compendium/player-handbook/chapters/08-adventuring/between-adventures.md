# Between Adventures

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 219-220
* Original section: Between Adventures

## Summary

Between adventures, characters can spend time and resources on lifestyle, crafting, professional work, recovery, research, and training.

## Key Rules

* Downtime converts narrative gaps into useful character activity.
* Lifestyle expenses describe standard of living and social context.
* Crafting, research, training, recuperating, and profession work can support future sessions.

## GM Use

Use downtime to seed hooks. A research project, repair job, contact, or training montage can point toward the next adventure.

## Common Rulings

* Ask each player for one downtime goal.
* Tie costs and time to the value of the outcome.
* Let complications create future scenes, not bookkeeping.
