# Damage and Healing

## Quick Rule

Apply damage to hit points after considering critical hits, damage type, resistance, vulnerability, and immunity; healing restores hit points up to the allowed maximum. Temporary hit points and temporary Force or tech points are tracked separately.

## When Used

Use whenever a creature takes damage, receives healing, spends hit dice, or changes hit point totals.

## GM Notes

Resolve damage before checking whether the creature drops to 0 HP.
