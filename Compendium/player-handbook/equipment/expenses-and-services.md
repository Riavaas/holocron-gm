# Expenses and Services

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 183-184
* Original section: Expenses and Services

## Summary

Expenses cover lifestyle, food, lodging, and services between or during adventures.

## Key Rules

* Lifestyle describes social conditions and comfort.
* Food, drink, lodging, and services support travel and downtime.
* Costs can matter for survival, contacts, and access.

## GM Use

Use expenses to anchor scenes in settlements and downtime.

## Common Rulings

* For routine costs, summarize. For scarce or high-stakes access, make it a choice or scene.
