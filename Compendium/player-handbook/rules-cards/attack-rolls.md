# Attack Rolls

## Quick Rule

Roll d20 + attack modifier and compare to the target's Armor Class.

## When Used

Use for weapon attacks, power attacks, opportunity attacks, and other effects that call for an attack roll.

## GM Notes

Check visibility, cover, range, advantage/disadvantage, and proficiency before the roll.
