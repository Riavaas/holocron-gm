# Dropping to Zero Hit Points

## Quick Rule

When a creature reaches 0 HP, apply instant death, unconsciousness, death saves, monster death, or knockout rules as appropriate.

## When Used

Use every time damage reduces a creature to 0 hit points.

## GM Notes

Pause and resolve 0 HP immediately. The correct path depends on creature type, damage overflow, and attacker intent.

SW5e Player's Handbook, pages 228-229.
