# Ability Checks

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 208-210
* Original section: Ability Checks

## Summary

Ability checks resolve uncertain tasks outside attack rolls and saving throws. The GM chooses the ability, decides whether proficiency applies, and compares the total to a DC or opposing check.

## Key Rules

* Roll d20 + ability modifier + relevant proficiency bonus if applicable.
* Use a fixed DC for environmental challenges.
* Use a contest when another creature actively opposes the attempt.
* Working together can grant help when the helper could reasonably contribute.
* Group checks resolve party-wide efforts where mixed success matters.

## GM Use

Ask for the player's goal and method before naming the check. This keeps the roll tied to fiction instead of becoming a button press.

## Common Rulings

* Call for Strength to force, Dexterity to finesse, Intelligence to reason, Wisdom to notice, and Charisma to influence.
* Use one roll for the whole declared approach; do not let every player retry the same failed check.
* For stealth, tracking, travel, or team tasks, use group checks when the party succeeds or fails together.
