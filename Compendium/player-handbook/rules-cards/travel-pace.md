# Travel Pace

## Quick Rule

Choose a pace for extended movement; pace affects distance, caution, stealth, and awareness.

## When Used

Use during wilderness travel, city movement under threat, patrols, infiltration, and chases.

## GM Notes

Make pace a choice: fast saves time, normal balances risk, slow supports caution.
