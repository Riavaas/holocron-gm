# Explosives

## Quick Rule

Explosives create area effects through thrown, placed, mined, or detonated devices.

## When Used

Use for grenades, mines, detonators, and sabotage scenes.

## GM Notes

Confirm blast area, trigger, save or attack, allies in area, and object damage.
