# Diseased

## Quick Effect

SW5e condition for sickness or infection effects.

## Mechanical Effects

* Track the disease source for symptoms, saves, and duration.
* Medical supplies or powers may remove it.
* Use Constitution saves when the source calls for resistance.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
