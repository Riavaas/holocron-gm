# Deafened

## Quick Effect

Cannot hear; sound-based perception and effects fail or suffer.

## Mechanical Effects

* Hearing-based checks fail or are impaired.
* Verbal warnings and sound cues may be missed.
* Does not automatically prevent sight-based actions.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
