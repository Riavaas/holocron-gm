# Restrained

## Quick Effect

Movement is locked down and attacks around the creature are affected.

## Mechanical Effects

* Speed is restricted.
* Attacks against the creature usually improve.
* The restrained creature attacks and Dexterity saves poorly.
* Track duration, source, save timing, and removal condition in the combat tracker.

## GM Use

Apply this as a visible status marker. On each affected creature turn, check whether the condition changes movement, action options, attacks, saves, or perception.
