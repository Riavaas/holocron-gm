# Passive Checks

## Quick Rule

Use a fixed check result, commonly 10 plus modifiers, instead of rolling.

## When Used

Use for hidden information, ongoing awareness, repeated effort, or when rolling would reveal too much.

## GM Notes

Passive Perception is the usual baseline for ambushes, hidden doors, and unnoticed threats.
