# Ability Checks

## Quick Rule

Roll d20 + ability modifier + applicable proficiency, then compare to the DC or opposed check.

## When Used

Use for uncertain tasks a character actively attempts outside attack rolls and saving throws.

## GM Notes

Ask for intent and method first. Choose the ability from the method, then decide whether a skill or tool applies.

SW5e Player's Handbook, pages 208-210.
