# Vision and Light

## Quick Rule

Light and special senses determine what creatures can see, notice, target, and hide from.

## When Used

Use in darkness, smoke, stealth, ambushes, night travel, enclosed ruins, and sensor-disrupting scenes.

## GM Notes

Track what each side can perceive. If a creature cannot perceive a target clearly, stealth and attack rules may change.

SW5e Player's Handbook, pages 217-218.
