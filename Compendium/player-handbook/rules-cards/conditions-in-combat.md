# Conditions in Combat

## Quick Rule

Conditions modify what a creature can do, perceive, resist, or suffer during combat.

## When Used

Use when powers, attacks, hazards, maneuvers, or features impose a named condition.

## GM Notes

When a condition appears in combat, look up the condition entry and apply it before resolving related attacks, movement, or saves.

## Source

SW5e Player's Handbook, combat chapter pages 221-230; condition details are indexed in Appendix A.
