# Contests

## Quick Rule

Both sides roll relevant checks. The higher result wins; a tie usually leaves the situation unchanged.

## When Used

Use when two creatures directly oppose each other, such as grappling, hiding, shoving, racing, or deceiving.

## GM Notes

The two sides do not always need the same ability. Match each roll to that creature's approach.
