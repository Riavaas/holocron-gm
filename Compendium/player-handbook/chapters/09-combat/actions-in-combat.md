# Actions in Combat

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 223-224
* Original section: Actions in Combat

## Summary

The action is the main choice on a turn. Standard actions cover attacking, casting, moving faster, avoiding danger, helping allies, hiding, readying a response, searching, guarding, and interacting with objects.

## Key Rules

* Attack is used for weapon strikes and other attack options.
* Cast a Power handles Force or tech casting in combat when a power uses an action.
* Dash increases movement for the turn.
* Disengage avoids opportunity attacks from movement.
* Dodge focuses on defense.
* Help supports another creature's task or attack setup.
* Ready prepares a response to a specified trigger.

## GM Use

When a player is unsure, offer the action menu in plain language: attack, move fast, get safe, help, hide, prepare, search, use something, or improvise.

## Common Rulings

* Improvised actions are allowed; choose a check, save, or contest that fits.
* Ready needs both a trigger and a response.
* Search in combat should find actionable information, not stall the scene.
