# Adventuring Gear

## Source

* Book: SW5e Player's Handbook
* Pages: 175-181
* Original section: Adventuring Gear

## Summary

Adventuring gear includes packs, ammunition, communications, data tools, explosives, life support, medical supplies, storage, utilities, and accessories.

## Key Rules

* Gear often avoids a roll or makes a roll possible.
* Ammunition and power cells matter for sustained action.
* Communications and data gear support investigation and coordination.
* Medical and repair supplies enable recovery or fixes.

## GM Use

Ask what gear the character uses before calling for a check.

## Common Rulings

* Gear can grant advantage, reduce DC, enable an attempt, or solve a minor obstacle outright.
